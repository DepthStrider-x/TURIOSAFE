Arunachal Pradesh

Land of the Rising Sun, Mountains & Monasteries

Overview

Arunachal Pradesh, in India’s far northeast, is a paradise of mountains, rivers, monasteries, and tribal culture. Known as the “Land of the Rising Sun,” it offers scenic beauty, peaceful valleys, and vibrant festivals.

Best Places to Visit

Tawang

Home to the famous Tawang Monastery, scenic lakes, and snow peaks.

Ziro Valley

UNESCO heritage site candidate, known for Apatani culture and music festival.

Bomdila

Charming hill town with monasteries and Himalayan views.

Namdapha National Park

One of the richest biodiversity hotspots in India.

Roing

Gateway to Mishmi Hills with rivers, valleys, and waterfalls.

Pros & Cons of Visiting

Pros

  • Pristine natural beauty and peaceful environment
  • Unique tribal cultures and festivals
  • Adventure opportunities (trekking, wildlife, rivers)
  • Less commercialized and touristy

Cons

  • Special permits (ILP) required for entry
  • Remote areas with limited connectivity
  • Fewer luxury accommodations
  • Challenging travel in monsoon/winter
